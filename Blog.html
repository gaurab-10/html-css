Impact of Technology

Technology is the use of scientific knowledge to solve practical problems. By looking at the history we can see how far we have reached in technology field. Technology can be viewed as an activity that forms or changes culture. Additionally, technology is the application of mathematics, science, and the arts for the benefit of life as it is known.

A modern example is the rise of communication technology, which has lessened barriers to human interaction and as a result has helped spawn new subcultures the rise of cyberculture has at its basis the development of the Internet and the computer.Technology has made human life comfortable more than ever. Using internet mobiles computer people easily connect with their nearest ones no matter where they are.Transportation has made the world like a one city people can move from place to place in shorter time like within a day around any part of the globe.Internet has helped a lot of students and teachers to learn and teach..... Internet is the sea of information every thing is found over the internet from household problems to education everything. Social medias changed the way of communication among individuals .From young to adult to communicate ,to pass lesiure time or to learn something they often use social media.In a few years, driverless cars may be the norm for everyone. There is no denying that the future of technology will continue to revolutionize our lives. Technology is influencing and transforming our world from every aspects entertainment, education, empowerment, awareness, health, communication, comfort.

Though technoglogy changed our lives it has also damaged the human life so badly.Digital technology means that vast amounts of data can be collected and stored. So, from cyber attackers or hackers people are receiving the threat calls regardimg leaking their personal images, audios, videos.Cyber bulling is also the next most dangerous thing over internet . Via social media, attackers attack the innocent young girls and make them victim of girls trafficking.There is an increasing tendency for people to socialize and communicate via digital devices rather than through real-life contact. This can easily lead to a sense of disconnect and isolation. Human beings have evolved over thousands of years to have real contact, so taking that away affects them in all sorts of negative ways that we're only just beginning to understand. Studies have suggested that the lack of real-life contact is causing depression and other forms of mental illness in many people.Copying and reproducing digital media is very simple. Schoolchildren can simply copy and paste their homework assignments without learning anything new.Addiction can be found in social networking, computer gaming, chatting, and dating websites where Users end up wasting a lot of time and money in exchange for a small return.Due to over exposure of internet proper parenting for children has become impossible.More time in pc or screen causes problem in blood circulation and health of individual,They snacks a lot and donot do exercises which will automaically causes a serious health problems.

Every thing has their own positive and negative aspects so we should try to grab the positive aspects to make ourselves better.
